# Tooltip

## Displays a tooltip that will help users in taking an action.

<Preview withToolbar>
    <Story name="Basic">
        <SCTooltip content="Click trigger Tooltip" position="right" trigger="click" autoCloseTime={5000}>
            Click Me!
        </SCTooltip>
    </Story>
</Preview>

<Props of={SCTooltip} />

### Content variant

You can use any kinda tag. Such as: `div`, `p`, `h1`

<Story name="Content">
  <div>
    <SCTooltip content="Tooltip" position="right" trigger="click" autoCloseTime={5000}>
        Click Me!
    </SCTooltip>
    <SCTooltip content="<h1>Tooltip with h1 tag</h1>" position="bottom" trigger="click" autoCloseTime={5000}>
        Click Me!
    </SCTooltip>
    <SCTooltip content="<p>This is tooltip's large content with p</p>" position="right" trigger="click" autoCloseTime={5000}>
        Click Me!
    </SCTooltip>
  </div>
</Story>

### Variant

6 variants are supported: `primary`, `primary-outline`, `success`, `success-outline`, `danger`, `danger-outline`

<Story name="Variant">
  <div>
    <SCTooltip content="Tooltip" position="bottom" trigger="click" autoCloseTime={5000} variant="primary">
        Primary
    </SCTooltip>
    <SCTooltip content="Tooltip" position="bottom" trigger="click" autoCloseTime={5000} variant="primary-outline">
        Primary Outline
    </SCTooltip>
    <SCTooltip content="Tooltip" position="bottom" trigger="click" autoCloseTime={5000} variant="success">
        Success
    </SCTooltip>
    <SCTooltip content="Tooltip" position="bottom" trigger="click" autoCloseTime={5000} variant="success-outline">
        Success Outline
    </SCTooltip>
    <SCTooltip content="Tooltip" position="bottom" trigger="click" autoCloseTime={5000} variant="danger">
        Danger
    </SCTooltip>
    <SCTooltip content="Tooltip" position="bottom" trigger="click" autoCloseTime={5000} variant="danger-outline">
        Danger Outline
    </SCTooltip>
  </div>
</Story>
